Centre for Fiscal Democracy: Our approach

Here is a very brief summary of the philosophy and approach we've evolved and hope to stick to:

  • prefer to do what is already known by experience to work; be blunt about getting things right, and acting strategically, rather than innovating
  • be gradual, tenacious, sustainable, long-term, concrete, practical
  • only accept funding that won't deflect us from our strategy; be happy to leave money on the table
  • prefer open source, open data, agreed protocols and interfaces, standards
  • form long-term relationships with civil society actors to scrutinise and engage with each state entity monitored
  • if individuals want only part of what we provide, don't make it unreasonably hard for them not to take the whole; they may combine our work with their own; accept that others get credit for changes we enable
